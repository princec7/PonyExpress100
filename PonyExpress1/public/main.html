The Pony Express Trail 100

Running in the hoofprints of history



50 and 100 Mile Endurance Run

This is your chance to run in the wild west on the historic Pony Express Trail in western Utah. The course is VERY remote and a support crew is required to provide support along the way. The course is 100% dirt road and only has about 3,000 feet elevation gain/loss.
